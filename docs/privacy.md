# Privacy Policy

**Auraninja** (the "App") is an open-source ambient sound mixer and internet radio application provided free of charge.

## Data Collection

**Auraninja does not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

The App operates fully offline for local sound playback. An internet connection is used only when you:
- Explicitly select an internet radio station to stream audio
- Fetch radio station metadata (name, cover art) from public endpoints

No analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking services of any kind are integrated into the App.

## Third-Party Services

The internet radio feature connects to user-specified streaming URLs and public metadata endpoints. These are external services with their own privacy policies. The App does not control or endorse any third-party content.

## Changes to This Policy

Updates to this policy will be reflected on this page. You are encouraged to review it periodically.
