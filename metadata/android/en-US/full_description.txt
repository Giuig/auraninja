Auraninja is a minimalist ambient sound mixer and internet radio app built with Material You design.

Features:

- Mix multiple ambient sounds (rain, birds, fire, wind, waves, thunder, cat, binaural beats)
- Internet radio support with Yumi Co. and Radio Browser
- Beautiful animated visualizers (Liquid Ribbons, Breathing Orb, Radial Rings, Particle Drift, Rotating Mandala, Constellation, Morphing Polygon, Aurora, Ink Diffusion)
- Binaural beats for focus, deep sleep, and relaxation
- Brown, pink, white, and green noise generators
- Sleep timer with countdown
- Volume control per sound
- Dark and light Material You themes
- Fully localized (English, Italian, French, German, Spanish, Japanese)
- No ads, no tracking, fully open source
